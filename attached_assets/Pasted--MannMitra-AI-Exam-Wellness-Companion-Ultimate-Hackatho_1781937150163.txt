# 🚀 MannMitra — AI Exam Wellness Companion (Ultimate Hackathon Prompt)

## 🎯 Mission

Build **MannMitra**, an AI-powered mental wellness platform for Indian exam aspirants (JEE, NEET, UPSC, GATE, CAT, CUET) that:

* Understands emotional patterns from journaling
* Detects hidden stress triggers
* Predicts burnout before it happens
* Acts as a deeply personalized AI companion

---

## 🧠 Core Philosophy

> “Understand. Predict. Support.”

The system must feel like:

* A senior who cracked the exam
* A daily emotional mirror
* A silent safety net

---

**MannMitra**

## ⚡ Core Differentiators (MANDATORY)

### 1. 🌍 Vernacular Intelligence Layer

* Support:

  * Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Bengali
* Input:

  * Text + Voice journaling
* AI:

  * Analyze emotion in native language
  * Detect stress type + intensity

---

### 2. 📊 Subject-Level Stress Heatmap

* Extract:

  * Subjects + events from journal
* Visualize:

  * Calendar heatmap
  * Stress vs subject trends

---

### 3. 🔮 Burnout Prediction Engine

* Analyze last 7–14 entries
* Output:

  * Risk level
  * Preventive suggestions

---

### 4. 🆘 Silent SOS

* Trigger via button or crisis detection
* Notify trusted contact
* Maintain calm UX tone

---

## 🤖 AI Companion Behavior (CRITICAL)

AI must adapt tone based on stress:

* Exam anxiety → structured help
* Burnout → recovery focus
* Comparison → reassurance
* Family pressure → validation

❌ No generic replies
✅ Context-aware responses
